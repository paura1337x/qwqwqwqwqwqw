Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining our fluffy paradise.

login

How do I join the server?

expand_more

It's super easy! If you are on Java Edition, simply use the IP kittylauncher.wuaze.com. For Bedrock players (Mobile, Console), use the same IP with port 19132.

devices

Is it compatible with Bedrock?

expand_more

Yes! We support full cross-play via GeyserMC. Whether you are on Java, Windows 10, Xbox, PlayStation, Switch, or Mobile, you can play together in the same world.

gavel

What are the server rules?

expand_more

We are a wholesome community. Our main rules are: Be kind, no griefing, no stealing, and keep the chat family-friendly. You can read the full rulebook in-game with /rules.

palette

Do I need a texture pack?

expand_more

It's not required but highly recommended! Our custom server resource pack adds cute furniture, hats, and a pink UI. It will prompt you to download automatically when you join.
