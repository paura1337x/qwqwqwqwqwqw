Zero Tolerance Policy

Using cheats (X-Ray, Kill Aura, etc.), damaging the server (Griefing), and behaviors involving severe insults are grounds for a permanent ban. No appeals are accepted for these matters.

sentiment_satisfied General Conduct Rules

Framework of Respect
expand_more

All players, administrators, and community members must be treated with respect. Discrimination based on religion, language, race, or gender, or making personal attacks, is strictly prohibited.

Disturbing Behaviors
expand_more

Trolling, stalking, or actions intentionally meant to ruin a player's enjoyment of the game are forbidden. "I was just joking" is not a valid defense.

sports_esports In-Game Rules

Griefing and Structural Damage
expand_more

Damaging other players' structures, breaking blocks, or making unauthorized changes is prohibited. Even if no claim has been set, respect others' work. Pouring lava or detonating TNT is strictly forbidden.

Cheating and 3rd Party Software
expand_more

Any modification or cheat software that disrupts the balance of the game, such as X-Ray, Kill Aura, or Fly Hack, is prohibited. Only permitted client-side mods like "Optifine" or "Litematica" (with easy place disabled) may be used.

PvP and Traps
expand_more

PvP (Player vs Player) is only allowed with mutual consent or in designated PvP arenas. Trapping players to kill them (TP trapping, etc.) is forbidden.

chat Chat & Communication

Spam and Flooding
expand_more

Filling the chat window unnecessarily, sending the same message repeatedly (spam), or constantly writing in all caps (caps lock) is prohibited.

Advertising
expand_more

Advertising other servers, YouTube channels, or commercial products is prohibited. Chat is for in-game communication only.

check_circle

Are you ready?

By logging into the server, you are considered to have read and accepted all the rules above. Have fun!
